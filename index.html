GET RECIPES FAST — NOT FAST FOOD.

Hello, we're YUMstack! Your go-to place for quick recipes. Simply enter in whatever you currently have in your kitchen and we'll give you great recipes to cook based on those items.

CUISINE PREFERENCE

In the mood for something in particular? YUMstack gives you the choice to filter by cuisine. Just enter in your items and we'll do our best to return yummy recipes catering to your current cravings.

IN A HURRY?

We know not everyone has time to spend hours cooking. That's why we give you the choice to search recipes by cook time. We have everything from grilled cheese sandwiches to osso bucco to stir fry.

HEALTHY RECIPES

Whether you're on a diet or just prefer to eat a more healthy meal, we have you covered. Go ahead and search by calories. Want a cheat day? We have bacon burgers and kimchi fries too!

ON A BUDGET?

No need to worry. Your satisfaction is our mission. Just search by budget and we'll give you recipes for cheap and tasty meals. If you want something a bit more elegant, simply adjust the recipe price accordingly.

HOW IT WORKS — SIMPLE AS 1, 2, 3

1

Simply go to the website or open up the mobile app and enter in the food items currently inside your refrigerator or pantry.

2

Search recipes according to your cravings and preferences and we'll give you a list of yummy recipes to cook.

3

Try the recipe. If you enjoyed it, go ahead and save it to favorites and share it with your friends and family.
